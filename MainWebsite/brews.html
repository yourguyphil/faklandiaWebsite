Roggenheim, Destroyer of Light

8.99$

Heroes vs Villains, the age old tale. Rye vs Wheat, the age old ale. This roggenbier is our strangely familiar take on a medieval style of beer from a time where good vs evil was an every day struggle. This beer is a delicate balance between dark and light, rye and wheat. Perfect spice and sweetness meld with dry finish and creamy mouthfeel to bring pleasant chaos. When good prevails, the light must be destroyed. Enter, Roggenheim, destroyer of light.
